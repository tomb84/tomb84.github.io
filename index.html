Welcome to my Github Site

As somebody who is interested in data, this is where I hope to use some Python and R code to do some web scraping and data analysis to write some interesting blog posts on some core interests of mine including running, travel and the environment.

A little bit about me.....I trained as an atmospheric scientist studying the composition of the atmosphere. I have experience running and developing global models of atmospheric chemistry and transport (written in FORTRAN). In a recent project I collected and analyzed trends in particulate pollution in Europe, North America and in the Arctic over the past 30 years. I have over five years experience analyzing and visualizing data with IDL, and writing Perl and Shell scripts to automate data processing. I have also achieved verified certificates in querying with Transact-SQL and statistical thinking for data science and analytics on the edX platform.

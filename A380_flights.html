The Airbus A380

The Airbus A380 is the largest passenger plane in operation in the world.

Which airports have the most daily A380 departures?

As of July 2016 the A380 was operating out of 50 different airports. The airport with the most daily A380 flights is Dubai with 64 daily departures. Dubai is the home hub airport of Emirates Airlines, which has the largest A380 fleet in the world. Second on the list is London Heathrow with 25 daily departures, followed by Singapore Changi (19) and Paris Charles de Gaulle (18).
